Abuja, Nigeria

Abuja is the capital and eighth most populous city of Nigeria. It is situated in the central part of Nigeria, within the Federal Capital Territory (FCT), and is a planned city built mainly in the 1980s. The city was developed based on a master plan by International Planning Associates (IPA), a consortium of three American planning and architecture firms. The Central Business District of Abuja was designed by Japanese architect Kenzo Tange.
