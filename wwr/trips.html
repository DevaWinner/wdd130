Ocean Rafting Trips

Welcome to our Trips page! Here you'll find detailed descriptions of our exciting ocean rafting trips.

Full-Day Rafting

This all-day tour combines water rafting with a tour of Ubud's highlights, such as the Monkey Forest, Rice Terraces, and High Swing. It's a great option to experience the thrill of rafting.

Bali Adventure Tour

This tour offers a full day of adventure in Bali, including white water rafting down the Telaga Waja and Ayung Rivers. It's one of the best things to do in Bali for adrenaline junkies

Ayung Half Day Tour

This half-day tour includes transport from Ubud and takes you white water rafting on the Ayung River. It's a popular choice for families with children or those looking for an activity closer to Ubud

River Adventure Combo

Experience the ultimate adventure with our River Adventure Combo. This tour combines white water rafting on the Ayung River with a thrilling canyoning expedition. Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping day!

Scenic Rafting Expedition

Embark on a scenic rafting expedition through lush tropical forests and breathtaking landscapes. This tour is perfect for nature lovers who want to combine adventure with stunning natural beauty.

Rafting and Cycling Tour

Get ready for a day filled with excitement and exploration. Start with an exhilarating white water rafting adventure and follow it up with a scenic cycling tour through the picturesque countryside.
