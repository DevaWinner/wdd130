Overview

Purpose

Our primary purpose is to market and promote our rafting business. By having a website, we will showcase our services, highlight our unique selling points, and attract potential customers.

Audience

Who are the target customers?
Our customers will range from teenage to adult outdoor enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and people who enjoy physical activities and nature, with a focus on individuals aged 18 to 45, who have moderate to above-average disposable income, as they will be spending on recreational activities.

How will my audience usually access my site?
Our audience will access our site through their mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. They will also reach our site through social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

What do they want?
Our audience wants to have a fun and memorable experience. They want to be able to enjoy the outdoors and have a good time with friends and family. They also want to be able to have a safe experience and be able to trust the company they are rafting with.
